15th birthday

April 22, 2020

For the past couple of months, people were stuck at home and my birthday went by at a blink of the eye. I didn't wake up until late in the morning, and nothing seems special for this day. I did what I do everyday, get up, grab something to eat, and all the stuff that I usually do. I almost forgot that it is my birthday, it just seems so... usual. Time sure flies by, and it is both rewarding and depressing. I feel like by this age I should know better, but I still feel like an ant looking up into the sky and wandering how it is so high up.
